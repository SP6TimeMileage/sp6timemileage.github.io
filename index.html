SP-6-Green-TimeMileage

Overview:

The app's objective is to track the milage of employees as they travel for work.
The app will generate reports based on an employee’s “trip” which can be used for getting travel credit from their employer.
It’s aimed at eliminating the guesswork involved in tracking milage for jobs.
